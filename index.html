About Information System

The Information Systems major is a scientific field that combines computer science with business and management. In this program you will learn how to identify the needs and business processes of the company based on the data owned by the company, then design a system that suits the needs of the company.

So, besides learning programming techniques, you are also required to learn the business processes in the company. Therefore, graduates of the Information Systems Study Program are expected to be able to develop a data processing system from various sources to be presented as useful information for the company.

Why Information Systems

Become a Wanted Programmer

Programmer professions are in high demand today, and the salaries offered are usually high. The Information Systems major provides the opportunity to study programming courses during college. For those who are serious about becoming a programmer, there is a great opportunity to master this skill.

Internship Opportunities at Startups

Opportunities to work in startup companies are now in high demand, due to attractive salaries, especially in the field of technology. Information Systems students also have the same opportunities, provided they have the necessary skills. An internship program before working at a startup can help you understand the work culture in that environment.

Combination of Computer Science and Business

The Information Systems Study Program is a blend of Computer Science and Business, which is highly relevant in the era of the Industrial Revolution 4.0. Graduates with an understanding in both fields will be highly sought after in various fields of work in the future.

Career Flexibility

One of the advantages of Information Systems is the opportunity to work in various sectors, not limited to technology companies. Nowadays, almost all types of companies need Information Systems graduates, giving you flexibility in choosing your future industry.

Opportunities in the Creative Industry

Apart from the formal sector, Information Systems graduates have great opportunities to work in the creative industry, for example as graphic designers or members of digital agencies.

Bright Career Prospects

With improved skills during college, you will have many career options when you graduate, such as becoming a programmer, analyst, web developer, application developer, project manager, data scientist, and others. Prepare yourself as well as possible during college.

Advanced Education Level

After completing a bachelor's degree in Information Systems, you have the option to pursue postgraduate education. Many universities offer Master's or Doctoral programs in Information Systems-related fields, both at home and abroad, giving you the opportunity to delve further into computer science.

Affordable Practicum Fees

Unlike other majors that may require expensive practicum fees, the Information Systems department offers relatively affordable practicum fees, maybe even free. The basic needs of practicum are usually met with computers that have been provided in the laboratory.
